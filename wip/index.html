I/O studio

We are working together to graphic and digital solutions through and design. We are based in Barcelona, and speak english, catalan and spanish. Read more →

Who are we?

We are designers and developers in favor of useful, lasting and innovative design. We are deeply curious, hypercritical, and share a passion for excellence in everything we do. We love to support charitable causes, non-profit organisations, and startups and we offer reduced rates for them.

Graphic

We work on projects ranging from brand identity and editorial design, to wayfinding systems and user experience design. We also collaborate with teams of complementary skills, including photographers, illustrators, and copywriters.

Digital

We create, design, and build websites and interfaces accross all digital platforms. Our approach is to make accessible and open top-notch applications. We connect to indie devs and teams to take demanding projects to the next level.

Work

We develop all forms of visual communication through a customized approach based on needs, goals and creative-based problem solving. Our work is built around the principles of simplicity and hacking and emphasizes both the look and functionality of each project. We love playing with new aesthetics and techniques, resulting in interesting pieces of work. Our self-initiated projects are under a Creative Commons License (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0).

Contact

We are currently taking on new projects. We enjoy working with businesses and individuals who think about, plan for, or consider the future, rather than just the present. If that is you, we’d love to hear from you. For further colaborations or more information and detailed case studies of our work, please get in touch.
