Projecte

We are a multidisciplinary team working together to create graphic and digital solutions through effective and design. We are based in Barcelona, and speak english, catalan and spanish.

Work

We plan, design, and develop cross platform and/or multi-media visual communication projects: advertisement, brand identity, editorial design, exhibitions, front end and back end development, graphic user interfaces, wayfinding, and web services.

We are designers and developers in favor of useful, lasting, and innovative design deeply committed to progressive enhancement and open source technologies. Our work is built around the principles of simplicity and hacking and emphasizes both the look and functionality.

We love playing with new aesthetics and techniques in self-initiated projects that we proudly share under a creative commons license (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0).

Contact

We enjoy working with businesses and individuals who think about, plan for, or consider the future, rather than just the present. We love to support charitable initiatives, non-profit organisations, and startups and we offer reduced rates to them. If that is you, we’d love to hear from you.

We also collaborate with teams of complementary skills, including photographers, illustrators, motion designers, and copywriters to take demanding projects to the next level. If you pertain to any creative field, and you feel the way we do, drop us a line!
