Work

We plan, design and develop cross platform and/or multi-media visual communication projects: advertisement, brand identity, editorial design, exhibitions, front-end and back-end development, user interfaces, wayfinding, and web services.

We are designers and developers in favor of useful, lasting, and innovative design and deeply committed to progressive enhancement and open source technologies. Our work is built around the principles of simplicity and hacking and emphasizes both the look and functionality.

We love playing with new aesthetics and techniques, sometimes resulting in interesting pieces of work and/or unexpected side projects that we proudly share under a creative commons license (CC BY-NC-SA 4.0).

We enjoy working with businesses and individuals who think about, plan for, or consider the future, rather than just the present. We love to support charitable initiatives, non-profit organisations, and startups and we offer reduced rates for them. If that is you, we’d love to hear from you.

We also collaborate with teams of complementary skills, including photographers, illustrators, motion designers, and copywriters to take demanding projects to the next level. If you pertain to any creative field, and you feel the way we do, drop us a line!

For further collaborations or more information and detailed case studies of our work, please get in touch.
